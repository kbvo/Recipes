Fried Rice

Description

When you ain't got nun to eat, this is always clutch

Ingredients

  • Rice (leftover/refrigerated if available)
  • Eggs
  • Oil (vegetable/high temp)
  • Soy sauce
  • Garlic (shallots too if u want)
  • Veggies (peas/carrots/corn)
  • Protein (shrimp/chicken/beef/pork)
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Madison Sqaure Garden

Steps

  1. Heat up pan/wok/griddle, oil it up, and aromatics (garlic & shallots)
  2. Cook protein till nearly done and season to taste (salt & pepper)
  3. Set aside protein and scramble eggs (add more oil if needed & season to taste w/ salt & pepper)
  4. Throw in rice, veggies, and everything else and whip it (add soy sauce till nice fried rice color)
  5. Serve on plate with Siracha
