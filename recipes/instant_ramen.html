Instant Ramen

Description

In case u threw away the wrapper

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Seasoning Packet
  • Water
  • Oil (may vary depending on brand)
  • Dehydrated topppings (veggies/bits of protein -> may vary DOB)

Steps

  1. Get serious and grab the wrapper out of the trash can and follow the instructions (stop playing with instant ramen like that cuz making it perfect lowkey an art form)
  2. Else if it really lost then boil up some water i guess
  3. Empty all the packets on the noodles in the same container and pour the boiling water over it (up to the line in the container if present or until the noodles start floating)
  4. Cover with lid and wait like 3 mins or unitl noodles are al dente-ish
  5. Serve with an egg if u feeling bougie or want extra protein (soft-boiled)
